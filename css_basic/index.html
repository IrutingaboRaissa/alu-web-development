    Values

    My top three important values for people to live peacefully.

    Based on a personal opinion

    A value is something that a person has and helps to live the way he lives.
    Values are important to for the people from similar or different backgrounds to live in harmony.

    1. Modesty : is having or showing a moderate or humble estimate of one's merits, importance, etc.
    ; free from vanity, egotism, boastfulness, or great pretensions.

    2. Modesty: is the ability to always tell the truth, never stealing or cheating. Not hiding the truth about something, being sincere. Being able to share honestly how you feel.

    3. Empathy: the ability to put yourself in someone else's shoes and imagine how or what they may be feeling.
    Empathic people understand that other people are effected by difficult situations and circumstances, so they make sure to be there for others and provide necessary assistance.
